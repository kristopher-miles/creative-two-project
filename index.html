How Much Wasted Time?

How much of your life have you wasted playing computer games? Enter your steamID and you can find out. Be warned: some things man was not meant to know.

@

Your SteamID is a unique 64 bit code that identifies your steam profile. You can extract it from your steam profile's url, or by logging into a service like STEAMID I/O. Your profile must also be set to public for this site to work.
